# Product Context

## Project Name
LEE Investment Handlers — Full Website

## Client
- **Company:** LEE Investment Handlers
- **Industry:** Wealth Management / Investment Firm
- **Offices:** Lagos, Nigeria + Venice, Italy
- **Tagline:** "Managing Wealth. Building Legacies."
- **Philosophy:** "Invest Wisely because Penny Wise..."
- **Engagement Type:** Fixed scope — full website build

---

## The Problem
High-intent individuals, families, and institutions seeking disciplined wealth management have no credible digital presence to evaluate LEE Investment Handlers. Without a professional website, the firm cannot establish trust, communicate its investment philosophy, or convert qualified prospects into consultation requests.

## The Solution
A full 7-page professional website that establishes institutional credibility, communicates the firm's investment philosophy and services clearly, and converts qualified visitors into consultation bookings — built on the Insighter design system (light mode, clean, authoritative).

## Target User
Three audiences:
1. **High-net-worth individuals** (35–65) seeking disciplined, personalized wealth management
2. **Families** planning multi-generational wealth and legacy
3. **Institutions** (foundations, trusts, pension funds) requiring institutional-grade asset management

All three are cautious, research-driven, and will leave immediately if the site feels untrustworthy, unclear, or generic.
